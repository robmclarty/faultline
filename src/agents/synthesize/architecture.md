## Instructions

Write a refined architecture specification that:

1. Describes the system's component structure in implementation-agnostic terms
2. Maps data flows between major components
3. Identifies boundary points and integration surfaces
4. Documents key architectural constraints and trade-offs
5. Incorporates cross-domain insights discovered during extraction

## Rules

1. Use abstract architectural language — describe components, boundaries,
   and data flows without naming specific technologies
2. Where the original architecture references specific frameworks or tools,
   abstract them to their functional role
3. Keep under 4,000 words
4. Focus on the "why" of architectural decisions, not the "how"
